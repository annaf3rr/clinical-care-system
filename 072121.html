Flow Chart

Algorithm

  1. Check your temperature and get dress.
  2. Decide if your going to clinic and consult your doctor.
  3. If No, drink alternative medicine and go home.
  4. If Yes, fill-out the clinic form and write all your symptoms.
  5. Wait for the availability of the nurse.
  6. Proceed to APE which is annual physical examination.
  7. Wait for the availability of the doctor.
  8. Doctor diagnosed the patient.
  9. Buy medicines that prescribed by doctor.
  10. Pay the clinical fee, go home and rest.

Psuedocode

BEGIN

BOOLEAN clinic_appointment

STRING patient_details

BOOLEAN nurse_available

OUTPUT "Kindly wait in the waiting room"

ELSE

END
